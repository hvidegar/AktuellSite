Welcome to Ekhagastiftelsen

 

Ekhagastiftelsen (Ekhaga foundation) was founded by Gösta Videgård as early as 1944 and since then we support scientific research in organic agriculture and complementary and biological medicine.


We are one of the larger foundations in Sweden who support scientific research and since 1999 we have awarded more than 189 million Swedish kronor to different research projects in agriculture and medicine.


On our website you can find information about which research we support, which projects have received support and how you apply for grants.
