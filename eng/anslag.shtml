Grants  

 

Ekhagastiftelsen (Ekhaga foundation) was founded by Gösta Videgård as early as 1944 and since then we have supported research in ecological agriculture and biological medicine.


Since 1999 we have awarded more than 113 million Swedish kronor to different research projects in agriculture and medicine.


On these pages we want to spread information about all the projects that we have founded. Today information is available for all projects since 2001. For project from 2004 and later there is even detailed information and such as a popular scientific project summary for all applications.
