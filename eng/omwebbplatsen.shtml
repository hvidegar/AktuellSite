Information about cookies

This website makes use of so called cookies. According to swedish law regarding electronic communication, which went into effect july 25 2003, all visitors to a website the uses cookies must receive information about:

» that the webiste uses cookies
» what these cookies are used for
» how cookies can be avoided

On the website of Ekhagastiftelsen cookies are only used for visitor stastistics.

If you do not accept the use of cookies you can change the security settings in your web browser. How to turn off cookies varies between browsers, and information about this can usually be found in the browsers help section.
