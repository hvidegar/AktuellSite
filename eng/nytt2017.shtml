New application instructions from March 23 2017

 

On March 23 2017 we made a large revision and update to our website. We have published more detailed information regarding what type of research we finance.


At the same time we have updated our application instructions so that it will be easier to apply for grants from us.


We ask that everybody starts using our updated application instructions, but we will not reject applications sent in using our old rules. Using our new information will have certain advantages compared to using the old instructions.


Simplifications

» Updated application form that is a PDF-form.

» Application to be sent by e-mail.

» Only the 2-page application form must be sent by regular mail.

» Better information regarding the research areas that are important for us.

» More information about what is important to include in an application.

» Updated budget instructions.
